# Tapp Merchant

Tapp Merchant is the merchant-side proof of work for Rails. It shows how a business can accept payment, see settlement state, and operate against Rails from a merchant workflow.

## What Tapp Merchant demonstrates

- Merchant onboarding
- Payment acceptance
- Live payment status
- Settlement completion

## Why it matters

It proves Rails is not just a backend settlement engine. It can support an operational product where merchants can actually use the rails in production-style flows.
